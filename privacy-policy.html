Privacy Policy

Utsanova Technologies Private Limited

Last Updated: 08 April 2026

1. Applicability and Scope

Utsanova Technologies Private Limited ("Utsanova", "Company", "We", "Us", "Our") is committed to safeguarding the privacy of individuals interacting with our platform.

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, processing, storage, and disclosure of personal data of:

  1. Users accessing our website and mobile applications
  2. Event participants and attendees
  3. Event organizers and vendors
  4. Service partners and contractors
  5. Employees, interns, and third-party stakeholders

This Policy applies to all information collected through:

  1. Our digital platforms (web and mobile applications)
  2. Electronic communications (emails, chats, messages)
  3. Offline interactions linked to our services

2. Consent and Acceptance

By accessing or using Utsanova's Services:

  1. You provide free, informed, specific, and unambiguous consent
  2. You agree to the collection, use, processing, storage, and disclosure of your information
  3. If you do not provide required information, certain services may not be available

Continued usage post-policy updates constitutes acceptance.


3. Permissible Age and Minor Data

  1. Services are intended for individuals 18 years or older
  2. Minors may use services only under supervision of a parent/legal guardian
  3. Where data of minors is provided, the provider confirms lawful authority and consent
  4. Data shall be used only for the specified purpose
  5. No profiling or marketing targeting minors shall be conducted

4. Categories of Information Collected

4.1 Personal Identifiable Information (PII)

  1. Full name
  2. Mobile number
  3. Email address
  4. Residential address
  5. Date of birth, gender (optional)
  6. Profile photo and account details

4.2 User Account and Profile Data

  1. Username, password (encrypted)
  2. Preferences, interests, event behavior
  3. Saved events, wishlists

4.3 User-Generated Content

  1. Reviews, ratings, feedback
  2. Photos, videos, comments
  3. Public contributions (visible to other users)

Such content may be displayed publicly at user's own risk.

4.4 Transactional and Booking Information

  1. Event bookings and ticket details
  2. Payment-related information (processed via secure third-party gateways)
  3. Billing details and transaction history

4.5 Event Participation Data

  1. Events attended, registered, or viewed
  2. Seat preferences, ticket categories
  3. Feedback and engagement patterns

4.6 Communication Data

  1. Customer support interactions
  2. Emails, chats, feedback forms
  3. Participation in surveys, contests, promotions

We may record interactions for quality control, dispute resolution, and fraud detection.

4.7 Device and Technical Information

  1. IP address
  2. Device ID, operating system
  3. Browser type and version
  4. App usage data, logs, timestamps

4.8 Location Information

  1. Real-time GPS data (if enabled)
  2. Location-based event suggestions

4.9 Contact Book Data (Optional)

  1. Contact names and phone numbers
  2. User confirms consent of such third parties before sharing

4.10 Images, Videos and CCTV Data

  1. Event photos/videos for promotional purposes
  2. CCTV footage for safety and security

4.11 Information from Third Parties

  1. Social login data (Google, etc.)
  2. Partner/vendor data
  3. Public sources and analytics providers

We may combine such data with internal records.

5. Methods of Data Collection

We collect information:

  1. Directly from users (forms, registrations)
  2. Automatically via cookies and tracking tools
  3. Indirectly through third-party integrations

6.5 Analytics and Research

6.6 Legal Compliance
