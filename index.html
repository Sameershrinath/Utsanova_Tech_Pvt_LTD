About Us

Our Team Brings Experience, Innovation, and Execution Together

Utsanova Technologies Pvt Ltd is a product-driven technology company building scalable digital platforms for event management and vendor ecosystems. We create integrated solutions for weddings, college fests, and corporate events - connecting users, vendors, and organizers through seamless, user-centric applications.

Our focus is on developing innovative, SaaS-based platforms that simplify operations, enhance experiences, and scale across industries.

To become a globally recognized technology company transforming the event and vendor ecosystem through scalable digital platforms used by millions.

We envision a future where event planning, vendor collaboration, and business operations are seamlessly connected through intelligent technology - making experiences smoother, smarter, and more impactful.

To build Utsanova into a unicorn startup by 2030 by developing innovative, scalable, and user-centric platforms that redefine event management across weddings, college fests, and corporate events.

We are committed to solving real-world problems through technology, empowering vendors, simplifying event execution, and creating value for users and businesses at scale.

We are not just building a company - we are building a future-ready digital ecosystem.
